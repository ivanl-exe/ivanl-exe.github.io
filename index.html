Biography

Hello, I'm Ivan; a [AGE]-year-old student whom from a very early age has had a fascination with computers. From exploring their usage to now having extensive experience in the underlying electronics, low-level management and programming. Always allured to new paths, among varied technologies; a breadth-first-search approach to life. Gradually exploding each node of unexplored sets available. From Arduino microcontrollers, to Solidity blockchains, to Pythonic and Rustaceous data science. Resulting in a comprehensive appreciation for that which we often take for granted. After all there must be some way in which we managed to magic sand into a Integrated Circuit?


Curiosity, a feature shared among all, the foundation of our need to learn. I am no different, with constant episodes of (eventually irritating) enquires? Life has no API call limit luckily.

> The important thing is not to stop questioning – Albert Einstein


Instinctive, this curiosity is what propelled by liking for STEM subjects, preferring to understand the real world; dismantling its very construct both scientifically and philosophically, although such has proved for both to be ever closer than I first imagined. With Dr Jordan B. Peterson inciting my desire to ever develop myself, to stand up straight among uncertainties.

> I don't tell people, 'You're okay the way that you are.' That's not the right story. The right story is, 'You're way less than you could be'. – Dr Jordan B. Peterson


Acceleration, my wish to ever strengthen myself has been exponentially increasing, with a rewiring for discipline. The art of doing what must be done within, with major progress both academically with consistent top grades and physically through body-building.
